Volunteer

Materials for the Arts provides volunteer opportunities for individuals, businesses, and community organizations. Volunteers at MFTA directly serve New York City's artistic and educational communities by performing tasks in the warehouse. Corporate team-building opportunities are also available.

Volunteer Types

Individuals

A great way for creative-minded people to show they care about the arts and the environment.

Groups

Fun, hands-on volunteering opportunities available for corporate groups, schools, and clubs.

Volunteering Activities

Warehouse Helpers

Help sort and display a variety of donated materials in our warehouse.

Shopping Helpers

Get to know our 4,200 recipient groups while they pick up supplies.

To discuss volunteering, email volunteer@mfta.nyc.gov or call 718-729-2125. To learn about past volunteering projects, visit Friends of MFTA.
