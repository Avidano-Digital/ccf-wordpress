Financial Contributions

Make a contribution that will help Materials for the Arts provide supplies and inspiration to thousands of New York City nonprofit organizations and public schools while keeping millions of pounds of useful materials out of the landfill each year.

“We don’t know where we would be without Materials for the Arts. We use everything we find to enhance our recreation centers. I love MFTA.”

Sonia Galarza, Director of Program Services
Administration for Childrens’ Services

Contributions can be made online, by check made out to Friends of Material for the Arts, or contacting Friends of Materials for the Arts at 718-729-2028.
